Projects

Final Bachelor Software Project

To complete my bachelor's degree, I worked on a project for the GeoScience department at the University of Utrecht. This project was undertaken in collaboration with thirteen fellow students, working as a SCRUM team. I dedicated a total of 430 hours to this project, which provided me with valuable experience in React and TypeScript. The project involved the development of a mobile application, a web application, and a server.

The objective was to create a mobile app that would make geological fieldwork more engaging and interactive. It allowed users to create points of interest on a digital map, containing photos and descriptions that could be edited and viewed by fellow students. The web application was designed for instructors to mark interesting points in advance.

Throughout the project, I primarily worked on the web application alongside two team members. However, at the beginning of the project, I underwent an extensive course on developing an Android app. Given that React is quite barebones, I had the opportunity to work with numerous libraries for this project.

This project has been a tremendous learning experience for me. I have enhanced my skills in testing and honed my ability to collaborate effectively within a large team setting.

Master Thesis

My master's thesis focused on "Actionable Test Suite Quality Assessment." I developed a tool that evaluates the quality of a Java test suite and provides feedback on potential improvements. The emphasis was on the feedback aspect, as it is often overlooked in similar research tools.

As part of the research, I examined the correlation between the quality scores generated by my model and those produced by another tool that also assesses quality. The results revealed a moderate correlation between the given scores.

Throughout this entire process, I learned to communicate my ideas more effectively, not only in discussions with supervisors but also in articulating these ideas and technical concepts in writing. Additionally, I improved my planning skills and became more productive in my work.
